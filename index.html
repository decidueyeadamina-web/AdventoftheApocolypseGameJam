What's a game jam?!?!?!

A Game Jam is an event for people to come together and make a game on their own or in a group based on a theme under a time limit! The theme of this game jam is Advent of the Apocalypse: Design the End of the World as You Know It! The game jam itself will have a production period of a week (March 7 - March 13), but you can do as much prep work as you want for the event before then!
